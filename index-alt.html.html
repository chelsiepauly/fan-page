Of Monsters and Men is a indie folk-pop band from Keflavík, Icleand. This band has five main members, along with other on tour members. Of Monsters and Men has a very unique sound, songs that lift you up, and the songs that everyone can relate to. Lead singer/guitarist Nanna Bryndís Hilmarsdóttir and co-singer-guitarist Ragnar "Raggi" þórhallsson create such a beautiful, one of a kind harmony when singing together, and also seperately.
